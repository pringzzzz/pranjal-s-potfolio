Best practices.

Four rules before you hit copy.

1
Optimise for mobile first
Most of your readers scroll on their phone — always check the mobile preview before posting.
2
No paragraph longer than 3 lines
Break up walls of text — short paragraphs keep people reading past the fold.
3
Remove em-dashes [ — ]
They read as AI-written. Use a comma, a period, or a line break instead.
4
Use a bright, high-contrast image
Bold, contrasty visuals stop the scroll in a busy feed — avoid dark or muddy images.
